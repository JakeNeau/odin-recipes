Slapped Chicken

Description

You all saw that popular internet post that did the rounds a while ago. With this simple recipe, you don't even need any equipment. All that is required is a raw chicken, your bare hands, and the sheer amount of will required to cook it through the power of your own slapping.

Ingredients

  • One raw chicken

Directions

  1. Unwrap your chicken and lay on a clean, flat, sturdy surface
  2. Slap the chicken roughly 23,034 times or until cooked
  3. Alternatively, you may slap the chicken once at 1,666 meters per second if you prefer it well done
  4. Serve hot
